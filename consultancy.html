hello!

i am offering an experimental new service in which you and i have one or more conversations regarding your creative process and goals, with a focus on the personal life-affirming satisfaction given by creative expression.

there are many things that can get in the way of this. i know because i have experienced many of these obstacles myself.

art does not, cannot, come first. many of these obstacles are perfectly unavoidable, even healthy, parts of life. others live in the mind. we will solve what can be solved and find balance together.

your life and your creative process are yours: you could do this alone and i believe in your ability to do it alone. in fact, it only works if you do most of the work yourself, for yourself. but i have found that, often, people enjoy and benefit from talking it through with someone else who gets it.

if you are interested in getting deep about why and how you make art, please reach out at the email address at the bottom of the page, or click here. i am offering this service at a temporarily very reduced rate reflective of the early stage of my new and uncertain practice.
